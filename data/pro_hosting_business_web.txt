#### [What is the difference between shared hosting and Business Hosting?](https://zthosting.com/business-web-hosting/\#1525786634845-6c63fede-1d1b)

The main difference between shared hosting and business hosting is the level of resources and performance they offer. Shared hosting is a cost-effective option where multiple websites share the same server and its resources, which can result in slower loading times and less stability.

On the other hand, business hosting is a dedicated hosting solution that provides businesses with server resources, ensuring high performance and reliability.
